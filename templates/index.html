About Anemia

A condition that quietly drains your energy

Anemia occurs when your blood doesn't have enough healthy red blood cells or hemoglobin to carry adequate oxygen to your body's tissues. It's one of the most common blood conditions worldwide, affecting people of all ages.

Left unmanaged, it can lead to persistent fatigue, weakened immunity, and complications during pregnancy or existing heart conditions. The good news: it's highly manageable once detected early.

  • Fatigue & weakness
  • Pale skin
  • Shortness of breath
  • Dizziness
  • Cold hands & feet
  • Irregular heartbeat
